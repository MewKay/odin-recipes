Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that typically consists of layered sheets of wide flat pasta, a tomato-based sauce, and various other ingredients such as ground meat, cheese, and vegetables. The layers are typically topped with a béchamel sauce and baked in the oven until the cheese on top is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground meat (beef, pork, or sausage) or vegetables for a vegetarian version
  • Cheese (usually ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan)
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Herbs and spices (such as basil, oregano, and garlic)
