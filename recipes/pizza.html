Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made from flattened bread dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flattened bread dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C).
  2. Roll out the flattened bread dough on a floured surface to form a pizza crust.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
